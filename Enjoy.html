ENJOYING OKINA YAMA

Whether you're seeking adventure or relaxation, our winter vacation destination offers a wide variety of activities to suit your preferences. In addition to skiing and snowboarding, we offer a variety of exciting activities to add some extra thrills to your winter vacation. Take in breathtaking views of the mountains on a helicopter tour or explore the surrounding wilderness on a scooter safari. For those who prefer a more leisurely pace, our spa facilities offer a range of treatments and therapies to help you unwind and rejuvenate. Whatever your interests, we have something to offer at Okina Yama.
