SKIING AT OKINA YAMA

Okina Yama is a spectacular mountain formation that offers some of the most challenging and exciting ski slopes in the entire world. With fantastic snow quality and slopes to suit all levels of skiers, from beginners to experienced skiers, this will be a skiing adventure that you won’t forget!

WE OFFER

LIFTS

We provide state-of-the-art lifts all over the mountain. Our lifts are designed to enhance your skiing and snowboarding experience, ensuring that you can reach the top of the mountain quickly and safely.

ALPINE

Our diverse range of skiing options caters to skiers of all levels, from beginners to experts. Whether you prefer groomed runs, challenging off-piste terrain or hitting the park, our ski resort has something for everyone.

CROSS COUNTRY

Our cross-country skiing tracks offer breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains. In addition, we provide rental equipment for cross-country skiing, making it easy for visitors to enjoy this popular winter sport.
